Q: Dave had 16 apps and 77 files on his phone. After deleting some apps and files he had 5 apps and 23 files left. How many apps did he delete?
A:Let's think step by step
To find out how many apps Dave deleted, we need to subtract the number of apps he has left from the original number of apps he had. Dave originally had 16 apps and after deleting some, he had 5 apps left. Therefore, Dave deleted 16 - 5 = 11 apps.
The answer is 11.

Q: Tiffany was collecting cans for recycling. On monday she had 8 bags of cans. The next day she found 7 more bags worth of cans. How many more bags did she have on monday than she found on the next day?
A:Let's think step by step
To find out how many more bags Tiffany had on Monday compared to the next day, we need to compare the number of bags she had on Monday to the number she found the next day. Tiffany had 8 bags on Monday and found 7 more bags the next day. Therefore, Tiffany had 8 - 7 = 1 more bag on Monday than she found the next day.
The answer is 1.

Q: Bobby had 21 pieces of candy. He ate 5 pieces of candy. Then he ate 9 more. How many pieces of candy does he still have left?
A:Let's think step by step
Bobby started with 21 pieces of candy. He ate 5 pieces first, so he had 21 - 5 = 16 pieces left. Then he ate 9 more pieces, so he had 16 - 9 = 7 pieces left. Therefore, Bobby has 7 pieces of candy left.
The answer is 7.
